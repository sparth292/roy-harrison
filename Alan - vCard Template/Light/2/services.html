Services

Service
Private & Semi-Private

Face-to-Face Consultations

In Face-to-Face Consultations, the client and I meet at an agreed-upon location. We chit-chat for a little while before focusing the session on identifying the problems/frustrations/difficulties they are experiencing. Next, I take some time to come up with a few ideas for solutions. Finally, we work together on a plan to turn these problems/frustrations/difficulties into learning experiences and viable solutions!

Service
San Diego & Southern California

In-Home Private Training

I travel throughout San Diego, Orange, Los Angeles, Imperial, and Riverside Counties to teach private and semi-private lessons in clients' homes. These lessons can be on self-defense, Jeet Kune Do, Filipino Kali, Kalis Ilustrisimo, Kickboxing, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, or any number of topics. Clients get the benefit of customized training programs without having to fight traffic.

Service
Multiple Disciplines

Instructor Courses

I offer Instructor Courses in Arrest & Control (5 levels), Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (10 levels), BJJ Over 40 (10 levels), Jeet Kune Do (12 levels), Filipino Kali/Eskrima (10 levels), and Harris Jiu Jitsu (10 levels). Each level focuses on different aspects of the discipline.

Service
Worldwide via Video

Online Consultations

Since March 2015, I've been doing online consultations on a weekly and monthly basis with students worldwide. We identify specific problems, I provide solutions with training methods, create lesson plans, and periodically review your progress.
